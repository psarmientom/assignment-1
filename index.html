The Life and Career of Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter born in 1989 hail- ing from West Reading, Pennsylvania. Taylor is known for her ability to cross genres in her music and her descriptive lyric writing

Early Life and Career

Taylor Swift moved to Nashville in 2004. After attending writing sessions with Elizabeth Rose, and catching the attention of new record label “Big Machine Records”, Taylor Swift began writing her de- but album. She signed a record contract with Big Machine, hired pro- ducer Nathan Chapman, and released her debut album. “Taylor Swift” in 2006.
